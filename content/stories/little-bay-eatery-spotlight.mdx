# Little Bay Eatery: Waterlooville's Favourite Brunch Spot

Looking for the perfect breakfast in Waterlooville? Look no further than **Little Bay Eatery** – a cosy café that's won the hearts (and stomachs) of locals with its freshly cooked food, friendly service, and homemade treats.

## What Makes Little Bay Special?

In a world of chain coffee shops and rushed service, Little Bay Eatery offers something refreshingly different: genuine hospitality and food made with care.

**"Delicious. We had a Little Bay traditional breakfast and poached eggs on toast,"** raves one customer. **"Yummy caramel latte and cappuccino (so yummy we got one to takeaway too!). Really friendly staff and lovely atmosphere."**

## The Menu

Little Bay serves up:

### Breakfast (9am - 11:30am)
- The famous Little Bay Traditional Breakfast
- Poached eggs on toast
- A range of hot and cold options

### Lunch (11:30am - 2:30pm)  
- Sandwiches, paninis & baguettes
- Toasties and hot specials
- Fresh salads

### Sweet Treats
- Homemade cakes that "looked scrummy" (customers' words!)
- Afternoon Teas for a proper treat

## More Than a Café

What many don't know is that Little Bay also offers:

- **Take-away service** – Perfect for when you're on-the-go
- **Outside catering** – Ideal for business lunches, events, and functions
- **Table service** – Sit down, relax, and let them look after you
