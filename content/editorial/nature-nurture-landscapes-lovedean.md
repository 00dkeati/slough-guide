Clare Wakeley has spent her life studying plants, teaching science, and running conservation projects across three continents. Now, she's brought all that expertise to Lovedean – helping Slough homeowners create gardens that are beautiful, sustainable, and teeming with wildlife.

Nature Nurture Landscapes isn't about neat rows and manicured lawns. It's about working with nature, not against it. Climate-resilient planting. Biodiversity. Gardens that give back.

## What Makes Clare Different

With a degree in Agriculture and the Environment, 15 years teaching science (including A-level biology), and hands-on conservation work from the UK to Australia to Thailand, Clare brings something rare: proper scientific understanding combined with practical experience.

## Services Offered

- **Garden Design:** From tiny urban plots to large rural gardens
- **Wildlife Areas:** Ponds, wildflower meadows, bug hotels, bird habitats
- **Climate-Resilient Planting:** Plants that thrive as weather patterns change
- **School & Business Projects:** Corporate team events and educational programmes
- **Maintenance & Training:** Ongoing care or teaching you to do it yourself
- **Foraging Walks:** Seasonal tours of the South Downs

## The Approach

Every project starts with understanding how YOU want to use your space. Clare practices wildlife-friendly methods, avoids synthetic chemicals, and even runs a plant share scheme where surplus plants go to other customers for free.
