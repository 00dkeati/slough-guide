Finding a good nail technician is a bit like finding a good hairdresser — once you've got one, you don't let go. J&D Nail Studio on London Road in Cowplain has clearly figured out how to keep people coming back, because they've been doing exactly that since 2012.

Over a decade in the same location, next door to Lidl, building a steady reputation one set of nails at a time.

## What They Do

The menu covers the full range of nail services you'd expect from a dedicated studio:

- **Acrylic Nails** — The classic extension for length and durability
- **UV Gel Nails** — Flexible, natural-looking, long-lasting
- **Shellac Nails** — The hybrid polish that actually stays put
- **Glitter Nails** — For when subtle isn't the goal
- **Pink & White** — The timeless French manicure look
- **Manicures & Pedicures** — The foundations of nail care
- **Nail Designs** — Custom art for those who want something unique

It's a straightforward offering, done consistently well. No gimmicks, no trends-of-the-month — just solid nail work from technicians who've been doing this for years.
