# Langley Guide 2026

Langley is one of Slough's most desirable suburbs, known for its grammar schools, good transport links, and community feel. Here's everything you need to know.

## Location & Transport

Langley sits on the eastern edge of Slough, close to the M4 and with excellent rail connections.

**Getting Around:**
- **Langley Station** - Elizabeth Line to central London (30 mins to Paddington)
- **M4** - Junction 5 nearby
- **Heathrow** - 15 minutes by car
- **Slough town centre** - 10 minutes

## Why Live in Langley?

**Pros:**
- Excellent schools (including grammar schools)
- Elizabeth Line connection
- Good mix of housing
- Community feel
- Parks and green spaces

**Cons:**
- House prices higher than Slough average
- Can be busy during school run times
- Limited nightlife

## Schools

Langley is particularly popular for its schools:
- **Langley Grammar School** - Selective, excellent results
- **Langley Academy** - Good comprehensive option
- **Primary schools** - Several good options

## Property

Average house prices in Langley run higher than Slough town centre. Good for families looking for more space and access to quality schools.
