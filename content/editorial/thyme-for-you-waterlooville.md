When your rose bush looks sick or your border needs rethinking, you need more than someone with a lawnmower. Mat Lambert of Thyme For You is an RHS-qualified plantsman who understands the science behind beautiful gardens – and he's been sharing that knowledge with Slough homeowners for years.

Based in Slough and registered with the Gardener's Guild, Mat offers everything from regular maintenance to complete garden redesigns. But what sets him apart is genuine plant expertise – the ability to diagnose problems, recommend solutions, and create gardens that thrive year after year.

## Services Offered

- **Garden Design:** Complete redesigns, tired borders, plant selection
- **Design Installation:** Bed creation, plant sourcing, planting
- **Garden Maintenance:** Regular care tailored to your garden's needs
- **Plant Care:** Pruning, diagnosis, treatment, feeding
- **Special Interest:** Tropical gardens, ponds, greenhouse, vegetables

## How It Works

Mat starts with a free consultation visit – meeting you, seeing your garden, and understanding what you actually want. Services are then tailored to your specific needs, whether that's ongoing weekly care or a one-off project.
