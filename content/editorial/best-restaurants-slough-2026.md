# Best Restaurants in Slough 2026

Looking for the best places to eat in Slough? We've compiled the top-rated restaurants across the borough based on Google reviews and local recommendations.

## Top Picks

### 1. MO:MO Mountain - Langley
**5★ (92 reviews)**

Authentic Nepalese cuisine that's taken Langley by storm. Known for their hand-crafted momos (dumplings) and flavourful curries. A must-visit for anyone who loves bold, spicy flavours.

### 2. Flavour Fix - Britwell
**5★ (23 reviews)**

A local favourite in Britwell serving up comfort food done right. Fresh ingredients, generous portions, and friendly service.

### 3. Bistro Saronida - Cippenham
**5★ (36 reviews)**

Mediterranean vibes in the heart of Cippenham. Great for date nights or family gatherings. Their grilled dishes are particularly popular.

### 4. Cafe Chai - Farnham Royal
**5★**

Authentic South Asian flavours with a modern twist. Perfect for a casual lunch or catching up with friends.

## Where to Eat by Area

**Langley**: MO:MO Mountain, various chains on the high street
**Cippenham**: Bistro Saronida, Noa's
**Slough Central**: Wide variety of cuisines along the High Street

## Tips for Dining in Slough

- **Book ahead** for weekend dinners at popular spots
- **Try the independents** - Slough has some fantastic family-run restaurants
- **Check Google reviews** - ratings are usually spot on
