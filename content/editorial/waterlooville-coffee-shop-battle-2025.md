Slough's coffee scene is thriving in 2025. With over 1,800 reviews across our top 5 coffee spots, we've analyzed the data to bring you the definitive guide to where you should be getting your daily caffeine fix.

## The Numbers Don't Lie

Based on real customer reviews and ratings, here's what the data tells us about Slough's coffee scene:

- **Costa Coffee** leads with 630 reviews and a 4.4-star rating - the go-to for consistency
- **The Old Forge Tea Room** in Hambledon scores highest at 4.9 stars (253 reviews) - the hidden gem
- **Poppins Slough** brings independent charm with 4.4 stars (306 reviews)
- **Chandlers** offers great value with 4.6 stars (260 reviews) - friendly service is their strength

## What Customers Really Want

After analyzing hundreds of reviews, the top customer preferences are clear: quality over quantity, friendly service, good value for money, and convenient location. It's not just about the coffee—it's about the experience.

## The Independent Advantage

While Costa dominates with volume, the independents like The Old Forge Tea Room and Poppins are winning on atmosphere and character. Reviewers consistently praise their friendly service, cozy atmosphere, and the personal touch you just don't get at chains.

## Our Verdict

For a quick, reliable coffee on your commute? Costa delivers. But if you want an experience that supports local business and offers something special? The Old Forge Tea Room (4.9⭐) and Poppins (4.4⭐) are where you should be heading.
