Some photographers specialise in one thing. Ania at Catching Dreams Photography has mastered several – from the intimate vulnerability of maternity sessions to the joyful chaos of family shoots to the emotion of wedding days.

Based in Southsea, she's built a following of over 6,500 on Facebook with her distinctive style that empowers clients while showing them their 'own unique shine.'

## The Approach

'My goal is to empower you through my images and show you your own unique shine,' Ania explains. Whether that's a glowing mum-to-be, a brand new family, or a couple in love, her approach remains consistent: relaxed, natural, and deeply personal.

## Services

- **Maternity photography** – celebrating pregnancy
- **Newborn sessions** – those precious first days
- **Family photography** – outdoor and studio options
- **Wedding photography** – full day coverage
- **Female portraits** – empowerment sessions
- **Brand photography** – for businesses and entrepreneurs
- **Makeover photo sessions** – transformation experiences

## Studio & Location Sessions

Ania offers both studio sessions and outdoor photography in stunning Hampshire locations. Whether you prefer the controlled environment of a studio or the natural beauty of the Hampshire countryside, she adapts to create the perfect setting for your photos.
