Every lawn is unique – and Handy Andy Lawn Care treats them that way. Based in Denmead and serving the whole Slough area, this lawn specialist brings professional-grade treatments to domestic gardens at reasonable prices.

The company believes correct mowing and watering aren't enough for a truly beautiful lawn. Their approach combines regular maintenance with targeted treatments – aeration, scarification, overseeding, and seasonal feeds tailored to Hampshire conditions.

## Services Offered

- **Lawn Treatments:** Fertilisation, weed control, moss treatment
- **Lawn Mowing:** Regular or one-off service
- **Aeration & Scarification:** Bringing tired lawns back to life
- **Overseeding:** Filling bare patches and thickening turf
- **Hedge & Shrub Trimming:** Complete garden care

## Eco-Friendly Approach

Handy Andy uses only high-quality equipment and eco-friendly products, ensuring lawn health without harming the environment. They provide customised solutions based on each lawn's specific condition and needs.
