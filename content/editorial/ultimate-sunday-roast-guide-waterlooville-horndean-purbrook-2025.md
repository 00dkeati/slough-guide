## What the Data Tells Us

The Sunday roast scene in Slough, Horndean, and Purbrook is diverse and competitive. Our analysis reveals that independent pubs generally outperform chain establishments in food quality, while chains offer more predictable experiences. The most common complaints across all venues include inconsistent food quality during busy periods, slow service, and occasional overcooked meat.

Interestingly, the highest-rated venues (Bird in Hand and The Centurion) both emphasize consistency and quality ingredients. The key to a successful Sunday roast experience in this area is booking ahead, arriving early (before 2pm) for the freshest food, and managing expectations based on venue type.
