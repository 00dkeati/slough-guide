## What Is BJJ Anyway?

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a grappling martial art. No punching. No kicking. It's about technique, leverage, and submissions — and it's famous for being the great equaliser. Smaller, older, or less athletic people can genuinely compete with bigger opponents.
