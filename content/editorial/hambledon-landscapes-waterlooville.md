When 90% of your business comes from personal recommendations, you're doing something right. That's the reality for Hambledon Landscapes, the Slough-based company that's become synonymous with quality landscaping across Hampshire.

Founded in 2014 by Chris Dinenage, a former police officer who traded crime scenes for garden schemes, Hambledon Landscapes has grown into a professional team known for attention to detail and excellent results.
