# Family Carpet Cleaning Business Celebrates 30 Years Serving Portsmouth

**A family-run carpet cleaning company established in 1992 continues to earn five-star reviews across Portsmouth and Hampshire.**

Albany Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning, now run by Neil, has served residents and businesses across Portsmouth, Slough, Havant and surrounding areas for more than three decades.

The company has built its reputation on quality workmanship, competitive pricing, and what customers describe as "magic" results.

"I was sure my sofa had died - but Albany were able to work magic. It's like new. Saving me £'000s! On time, friendly, honest. Thanks very much!" wrote one impressed customer.

Another reviewer highlighted the value: "Fabulous job, carpets look amazing. Very quick and affordable. Thank you."

Neil has become known for his honest approach and quick response times. "We have used Neil over the years and he has never let us down. Punctual, very efficient and helpful & does a first class job," said a long-standing customer.

The business offers transparent, published pricing - a single room from £40, stairs from £40, and upholstery from £15 per item. Larger jobs receive discounts, with no minimum spend and no VAT.

Albany serves a range of commercial settings including offices, care homes, pubs and restaurants, as well as residential properties. The company also handles end-of-tenancy cleaning and fumigation.

Neil is known for providing immediate telephone quotes and honest advice, often responding to enquiries within minutes.
