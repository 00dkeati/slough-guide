# Best Cafes in Slough 2026

Whether you need your morning flat white or a quiet spot to work, Slough has some brilliant cafes. Here are the top-rated spots.

## Top Cafes

### 1. Bistro Saronida - Cippenham
**5★ (36 reviews)**

More than just a cafe - great coffee, fantastic food, and a lovely Mediterranean atmosphere. Perfect for brunch.

### 2. G&G Coffee - Chalvey
**5★ (24 reviews)**

Proper speciality coffee in Chalvey. The baristas know their stuff, and the beans are always freshly roasted.

### 3. Burnham Bites - Burnham
**5★ (22 reviews)**

A Burnham institution. Great coffee, delicious cakes, and a warm welcome every time.

### 4. Noa's - Cippenham
**5★ (12 reviews)**

Cosy neighbourhood cafe with excellent pastries and friendly staff.

### 5. Laugh The Latte - Burnham
**5★**

Quirky name, serious coffee. A newer addition that's quickly building a loyal following.

## Chains Worth Mentioning

- **Costa** - Multiple locations across Slough
- **Starbucks** - Slough High Street
- **Caffe Nero** - Near the station
- **Greggs** - For a quick coffee and sausage roll

## Best for...

- **Working**: G&G Coffee (good wifi, power sockets)
- **Brunch**: Bistro Saronida
- **Quick catch-up**: Any of the High Street chains
- **Cake lovers**: Burnham Bites
