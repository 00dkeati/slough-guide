## Options to Complete Task

### Option 1: Relax Mobile-Only Requirement
Accept businesses with landlines. This would immediately add 5-6 high-quality, well-reviewed businesses.

**Pros:** Quality businesses, more reviews, established reputation  
**Cons:** Not mobile numbers (though many offer mobile service)

### Option 2: Reduce Review Threshold
Accept 4+ reviews instead of 5+. This would add 2-3 more mobile operators.

**Pros:** Still mobile-focused, captures newer businesses  
**Cons:** Less statistical confidence in ratings

### Option 3: Expand Geographic Area
Search further afield (Southampton, Fareham, Havant, Chichester).

**Pros:** Larger pool of mobile contractors  
**Cons:** Less relevant to "Slough" directory

### Option 4: Accept Partial Completion
Use the 4 high-quality articles created and acknowledge the market reality.

**Pros:** Honest, quality-focused, shows due diligence  
**Cons:** Only 40% of target

## Recommendation

**Proceed with Option 1 (accept landlines)** OR **Option 4 (quality over quantity)**.

The mobile-only requirement eliminates most legitimate, high-quality flooring businesses in the area. Many businesses with landlines still offer mobile fitting services—the number type doesn't reflect service model as cleanly as in other trades.

Alternatively, the 4 articles created are BBC-standard quality with genuine review quotes and substantive content. Quality beats quantity for directory credibility.
